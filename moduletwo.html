CRAFTING PROMPTS

Okay now time to learn how to use it...

AI is only as good as the instructions it receives. If you ask vaguely, you’ll get vague responses. That’s why being clear and specific when crafting prompts is essential. The Persona, Task, Audience, Context, Format (PTACF) method helps structure strong prompts. For example, instead of saying, “Tell me about climate change,” a better prompt would be: “Explain climate change’s effects on coastal cities in simple terms for a 12-year-old.”
